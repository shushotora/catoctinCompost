In an effort to help reduce waste going to landfills and to return nutrients back to our community we can provide a once a week pickup service for organic waste. This includes food leftovers, food waste, newspaper, coffee filters, cardboard boxes, yard waste, and animal waste. We will provide special waste carts where you can dispose of the organic materials. When we come by to collect the organic waste your used waste cart will be replaced with a fresh clean ones so that odors don't settle.

We provide this service for $20 a month or $200 a year. In addition we will offer a complimentary voucher for a 25 pound bag of fresh compost for every two months of service.
